About MoodTrack

MoodTrack is a web application designed to help users track their emotional patterns over time and understand how external factors like weather may influence their mood.

Problem

Many people experience changes in mood throughout the day or week but do not consistently track them. This makes it difficult to identify patterns related to stress, environment, or daily routines.

Solution

MoodTrack allows users to log their daily mood along with notes and automatically connects each entry with real-time weather data using the Open-Meteo API.

Who This Is For

  • Students managing stress
  • Mental wellness users
  • Counselors and advisors

How It Works

  • Log mood (1–10)
  • Fetch weather automatically
  • Store in Supabase
  • View trends on dashboard
